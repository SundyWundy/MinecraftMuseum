Minecraft Museum Mobs Catalog

Mob Texture Description
Bat Bats are ambient flying passive mobs that spawn in caves.
Bee Bees are flying neutral mobs that live in bee nests and beehives. If provoked, bees attack in a swarm to "sting" the player and inflict poison.
Blaze Blazes are flying hostile mobs found in nether fortresses.
Cat Cats are tameable passive mobs that are found in villages and swamp huts.
Cave Spider Cave spiders are small, venomous variants of spiders that live in abandoned mineshafts.
Chicken Chickens are common passive mobs found in grassy biomes.
Chicken Jockey Chicken jockeys are the rare appearance of a baby zombie, baby zombified piglin, baby zombie villager, baby husk, or baby drowned riding a chicken.
Cod Cod are common passive fish mobs found in oceans.
Cow A cow is a common passive mob, source of leather, raw beef and milk. Cows are found in grassy biomes.
Creeper A creeper is a common hostile mob that silently approaches players and explodes. Due to their distinctive appearance and high potential for killing unwary players as well as damaging the environment and players' constructions, creepers have become one of the icons of Minecraft, both among players and non-players. Creepers are a major source of gunpowder as well as the only way to obtain most music discs. When struck by lightning, a creeper becomes charged, which amplifies its explosion power and enables mob heads to be obtained.
Dolphin Dolphins are neutral mobs that live in non-frozen oceans.
Donkey A donkey is a mob that can be ridden when tamed.
Drowned Drowned are common underwater zombie variants that spawn in water or when a zombie drowns. They are also the only source of tridents.
Elder Guardian An elder guardian is a stronger, larger variant of the guardian that can inflict Mining Fatigue. Three of them spawn inside an ocean monument; one in the penthouse room and one in each wing.
Ender Dragon The Ender dragon is a dangerous, flying hostile boss mob found when first entering the End. She is the largest naturally spawning mob in the game, and is widely acknowledged as the main antagonist and final boss of Minecraft.
Enderman Endermen are tall, black neutral mobs from the End that have the ability to teleport, and pick up blocks.
Endermite Endermites are the smallest hostile mobs in Minecraft.
Evoker An evoker is a spell-casting illager found in woodland mansions and raids, and is the only source of the totem of undying.
Fox Foxes are nocturnal passive mobs that spawn commonly in taiga, giant tree taiga, and snowy taiga biomes. They move to villages during the night.
Ghast Ghasts are large white floating jellyfish-like hostile mobs that live in the Nether which shoot explosive fireballs at the player.
Guardian A guardian is pufferfish-like hostile mob that spawn in and around ocean monuments. They attack players and squid with a laser beam that cannot be dodged or blocked by a shield.
Hoglin Hoglins are warthog-like breedable hostile mobs and the main source of food in the Nether.
Horse Horses are passive mobs that can be ridden when tamed.
Husk Husks are variants of zombies that spawn in deserts. Unlike zombies, husks do not burn in sunlight.
Iron Golem An iron golem is a large, strong, neutral utility mob that defends players and villagers.
Llama Llamas are neutral mobs that can be tamed and used to transport large shipments of items.
Magma Cube Magma cubes are hostile mobs whose skin is dark red and black with red, orange, and yellow eyes. They are found in big, small, and tiny sizes, exclusively in the Nether. They are the Nether equivalent of slimes, with similar behavior.
Mooshroom Mooshrooms are mushroom-covered variants of cows exclusive to the rare mushroom fields biome.
Mule A mule is a tameable mob that can be ridden when tamed.
Ocelot Ocelots are rare passive mobs found in jungles.
Panda A panda is a rare mob that resides in jungles whose behavior is neutral, but can sometimes be passive or hostile. Baby pandas and pandas that enter love mode are passive to players. Adult pandas are neutral to players.
Parrot Parrots are rare tameable mobs found in jungles. Parrots can imitate sounds of nearby hostile mobs and can perch on the player's shoulders.
Phantom Phantoms are common undead stingray-like hostile mobs that spawn when the player has not attempted to enter a bed for three or more in-game days.
Pig A pig is a common passive mob found in grassy biomes, which drops porkchops and can be ridden with a saddle.
Piglin Piglins are neutral mobs found in the Nether. Unless equipped with a piece of golden armor, they attack players on sight. Players can use gold ingots to barter with them for various items.
Piglin Brute A piglin brute is a hostile, stronger variant of piglins that appears in all types of bastion remnants.
Pillager Pillagers are hostile illagers armed with crossbows.
Polar Bear Polar bears are neutral mobs that live in icy biomes.
Pufferfish Pufferfish are mobs found in oceans. They defend themselves by dealing damage and inflicting poison to nearby players and certain mobs, but do not become hostile.
Rabbit A rabbit is an uncommon passive mob. Rabbits are a source of rabbit's foot, rabbit hide, and rabbit meat.
Ravager Ravagers are large bovine hostile mobs that spawn in raids and attack players, villagers, wandering traders and iron golems.
Ravager Jockey Ravager jockeys are formed when a Pillager, Vindicator or an Evoker rides on a Ravager.
Salmon Salmon are common passive mobs found in oceans and rivers.
Sheep Sheep are common passive mobs found in many of the grassy biomes that supply wool and mutton.
Shulker Shulkers are box-shaped hostile mobs found in end cities. They hide in their shell to blend in with surrounding purpur blocks and guard the city's various treasures.
Silverfish Silverfish are small, bug-like hostile mobs found in strongholds, igloo basements, and mountains.
Skeleton Skeletons are common undead hostile mobs equipped with bows.
Skeleton Horse Skeleton horses are undead variants of regular horses.
Slime Slimes are bouncy cube-shaped hostile mobs that spawn in the swamp and, occasionally, deep underground in particular chunks called slime chunks.
Snow Golem A snow golem is a snowman-like utility mob that helps defend against hostile mobs.
Spider Spiders are common neutral mobs that have the unique ability to climb walls.
Spider Jockey Spider jockeys are the rare appearance of a spider or cave spider being ridden by a skeleton, stray, or wither skeleton.
Squid Squid are passive mobs that spawn in water.
Stray Strays are variants of skeletons that spawn only in frozen, ice, or snowy biomes.
Strider Striders are passive mobs native to the Nether. They can walk/stride on lava and be saddled by the player. A warped fungus on a stick is needed to control a strider, similar to how a pig is controlled by a carrot on a stick.
Tropical Fish Tropical Fish are common passive mobs found in oceans. There are 3,584 possible variants.
Turtle A turtle is a common passive mob found in beach and ocean biomes.
Vex Vexes are flying hostile mobs summoned in small groups by the spell-casting evoker.
Villager Villagers are passive mobs that inhabit villages, work at their professions, breed, and interact. Their outfit varies according to their occupation and biome. A player can trade with villagers using emeralds as currency.
Vindicator Vindicators are illagers equipped with iron axes.
Wandering Trader The wandering trader is a passive mob found randomly across the world. It can trade, making natural items more available, less dangerous to obtain, and in some cases, renewable.
Witch Witches are uncommon hostile mobs that use harmful splash potions as ranged weapons and beneficial potions on themselves defensively. While dangerous, they are a source of valuable potion ingredients.
Wither The wither is an undead hostile boss mob that floats and shoots explosive skulls at players and mobs. It is currently the only player-made hostile mob in the game. The fight varies across editions; the Bedrock wither has twice as much health and different attacks than its Java counterpart. The wither drops 50 experience points and a nether star, a crafting ingredient for beacons.
Wither Skeleton Wither skeletons are hostile mobs and variants of Skeletons with stone swords found in nether fortresses.
Wolf Wolves are neutral mobs that can be tamed.
Zoglin Zoglins are undead hostile mobs created when a hoglin enters the Overworld or End.
Zombie Zombies are common undead hostile mobs that attack players, villagers, wandering traders, iron golems, snow golems, baby turtles, and stomp on turtle eggs. They burn in sunlight, similar to most other undead mobs.
Zombie Villager Zombie villagers are variants of zombies that can be cured into normal villagers using golden apples and under the effect of Weakness.
Zombified Piglin Zombified piglins are undead, neutral mobs that mainly inhabit the Nether. A zombified piglin becomes hostile when it or another nearby zombified piglin is attacked.
