Minecraft Museum Items Catalog

Tools
Item Name Item Texture Description
Pickaxe Pickaxes are one of the most commonly used tools in the game, being required to mine all ores, rock, rock-based blocks and metal-based blocks. They allow the player to mine blocks at faster speeds, and the speed increases depending on the material it is made out of.
Axe An axe is a tool used to hasten the collection of wood-based items. It is also used as a weapon for melee attacks.
Sword A sword is a melee weapon, that is mainly used to deal damage to mobs and/or players. Swords are also useful for mining cobwebs and bamboo. A sword is made from one of six materials, in order of increasing quality and expense: wood, stone, gold, iron, diamond, and netherite.
Shovel Shovels are tools used to ease the process of collecting dirt and other blocks.
Hoe Hoes are tools used to till dirt and grass blocks into farmland, as well as quickly harvest certain blocks.
Map A map is an item used to view explored terrain and mark landmarks.
Flint and Steel A flint and steel is a tool used to light fires.
Shears Shears are a tool used primarily to shear sheep to get wool and mine a few types of blocks.
Shield A shield is a tool used for protecting the player against attacks.
Compass Compasses are an item used to point to the world spawn.
Bow A bow is a ranged weapon that fires arrows.
Bucket Buckets are tools used to carry water, lava, milk, and fish (cod, pufferfish, salmon, and tropical fish only.)
Fishing Rod A fishing rod is a tool used primarily for fishing.

Minerals and Ingots
Item Name Item Texture Description
Coal Coal is an item mainly obtained by harvesting coal ore blocks. It is used as fuel, for crafting torches and campfires, or for trading with villagers.
Iron Ingot Iron ingots are versatile metal ingots used extensively in crafting.
Lapis Lazuli Lapis lazuli is a mineral used for enchanting and decoration.
Redstone Dust Redstone dust is a mineral that can transmit redstone power when placed as a block.
Gold Ingot Gold ingots are metal ingots used to craft various items and used as currency for bartering with piglins.
Diamond A diamond is a rare mineral obtained from diamond ore or loot chests. They are mainly used to craft high-tier tools and armor, enchanting tables, blocks of diamond, and jukeboxes.
Emerald Emeralds are rare minerals, and are used to trade with villagers or to power beacons. They are obtained from emerald ore, one of the rarest ores in Minecraft.
Nether Quartz Nether quartz is a smooth, white mineral found in the Nether.
Netherite Scrap Netherite scrap is a material smelted from ancient debris, which is found in the Nether. Its sole use is for crafting netherite ingots. Like most netherite-related items, it does not burn in fire or lava.
